Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about Wiki Jet? Here are answers to the most common things new pilots ask.

What flight simulators does Wiki Jet support?
Wiki Jet currently supports Microsoft Flight Simulator and X-Plane for flight tracking and virtual airline operations.
Do I need to be an experienced virtual pilot to join?
Nope — beginners are welcome! Our goal is to create a relaxed, fun, and beginner-friendly environment where you can learn and enjoy inter-island flying.
Is there a required schedule or minimum number of flights?
There are no mandatory flight activity requirements. Fly when you want, as often as you want — this VA is designed for casual hobbyists.
Do flights have to stay within Hawaii?
Our primary network focuses on Hawaiian inter-island operations, but pilots are welcome to explore additional routes for fun as well.
How do I log my flights?
Flights are logged automatically through our VA platform once connected. After landing, your flight will appear in your pilot dashboard.
