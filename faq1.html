Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about flying with Transpacific Virtual Airlines.

What simulator platforms are supported?

We currently support Microsoft Flight Simulator and X-Plane. Pilots can also file manual PIREPS, which means we welcome members on XBOX and Playstation!

Do I need to be an experienced pilot to join?

No — pilots of all experience levels are welcome. We're happy to help you get started!

Is there a minimum activity requirement?

This is a hobby that is meant to be fun. You will not find activity requirements at Wiki Jet.

Which aircraft does Wikijet operate?

We currently operate the A320neo, but pilots may fly any aircraft in the A320 family. We have plans to expand our fleet in the future.

How are flights tracked?

Flights are logged using LRM, which records flight data automatically when connected.
