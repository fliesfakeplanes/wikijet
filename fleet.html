The Wiki Jet A320neo

The Airbus A320neo is the sole aircraft operated by Wiki Jet. Its versatility and efficiency allow pilots to fly a wide range of routes while keeping operations simple and consistent.

Aircraft Overview

Aircraft: Airbus A320neo

Role: Short & medium-haul operations

Range: Regional to transcontinental

Philosophy: One aircraft, many missions

Why the A320neo?

By standardizing on a single aircraft type, Wiki Jet removes unnecessary barriers to entry. Pilots can focus on learning one set of systems and enjoying meaningful flying across the network.

Wiki Jet Livery

The Wiki Jet livery is a fictional design created exclusively for our virtual airline. It reflects a clean, modern identity and gives pilots a consistent visual presence wherever they fly.
