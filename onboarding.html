Pilot Onboarding Steps

Follow these steps to get started with Wiki Jet.

1. Create Your Pilot Profile ▶

Create your profile with your name, preferred callsign, and simulator setup.

Make sure your email is valid — it’s how we’ll contact you.

2. Join Our Discord ▶

Access community announcements, help channels, and fleet resources by joining our Discord server.

3. Complete Required Training ▶

Review the basic flight operations, SOPs, and ACARS procedures.

Mark the training as complete in your pilot profile when you’re ready.

4. Link Your Flight Tracker ▶

Connect your preferred flight tracker (like VATSIM or IVAO) to your Wiki Jet profile so your flights log automatically.

5. Submit Your First Flight ▶

Fly your first official flight and submit it through the system. Congrats — you’re live!
